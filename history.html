Professional Profile

Dr. Rohit Bhatnagar is a distinguished leader with over 36 years of transformative expertise in safety engineering, specializing in lightning protection, CBRN security, and AI-driven automation.

Pioneering Journey

In 2001, Dr. Bhatnagar identified critical vulnerabilities in surge protection amid India's rapid electronics adoption. His leadership transformed the company into an industry pioneer, delivering fail-safe lightning/surge mitigation systems that protect critical infrastructure nationwide. Today, his youth-mentored team continues to lead the sector through relentless R&D in safety technologies.


Dr. Rohit Bhatnagar established Pragati Engineers in 2001 with a vision to provide innovative, reliable, and high-quality engineering solutions. Under his leadership, the company has grown from a small start-up into a trusted name in the field, delivering excellence across projects in safety, security systems, and specialized engineering solutions.


With over two decades of commitment, Mr. Bhatnagar has been instrumental in building a strong foundation of trust, quality, and customer satisfaction. His forward-thinking approach and dedication to innovation continue to drive Pragati Engineers toward new milestones.
