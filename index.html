About Us

Driven by Vision. Powered by Expertise.

Founded in 2001 by Dr. Rohit Bhatnagar, Pragati Engineers is a trusted name in delivering innovative, high-quality, and time-bound engineering solutions. With two decades of excellence, we specialize in CCTV Surveillance, AV Systems, Lightning Protection, CBRN Safety, and Disaster Management.

Backed by a skilled team of engineers and project experts, we ensure end-to-end execution — from design to integration. Our commitment lies in building safe, smart, and secure infrastructure with unmatched quality and reliability.

At Pragati Engineers, we don’t just deliver systems — we deliver lasting solutions.

Our Services

Technology & Communication Solutions

At Pragati Engineers, we deliver advanced technology solutions that empower smarter communication, stronger security, and more connected environments.

CCTV Surveillance Systems

State-of-the-art surveillance solutions designed for real-time monitoring, deterrence, and incident analysis. We provide complete end-to-end integration for high-security environments.

Audio Visual Systems

Immersive and reliable AV setups for corporate, government, and public spaces — delivering clarity, control, and seamless interaction in every setting.

Conference Systems

Modern wired and wireless conferencing solutions for secure, high-quality communication in boardrooms, training halls, and control centres.

IT & Software Solutions

Tailored IT infrastructure and custom software development services that support secure networking, system integration, automation, and operational efficiency.

Smart Education Solutions

Interactive classroom technology that enhances digital learning experiences and boosts engagement through smart boards, content delivery tools, and remote learning platforms.

Lightening and Surge Protection

We provide reliable lighting solutions for enhanced visibility and aesthetics. Our surge protection systems secure equipment from power fluctuations, ensuring smooth and safe operation.

Safety, Disaster Management & Infrastructure Systems

Our solutions are designed to protect lives, critical infrastructure, and operations from natural and man-made disasters through proactive technologies and intelligent systems.

CBRN Safety Solutions

Specialized in high-stakes environments, we deploy solutions to detect, contain, and respond to Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear incidents. These systems meet national and international safety protocols.

Early Earthquake Warning Systems

We provide fast-acting seismic sensors and automated alert systems that give critical seconds of advance warning — enabling timely evacuation and activation of safety protocols.

Lightning Protection Systems

Engineered to international standards, our lightning protection systems are designed to shield buildings, equipment, and personnel from the devastating effects of lightning strikes.

Building Management Systems (BMS)

Our intelligent BMS solutions integrate HVAC, lighting, fire safety, and security systems into one centralized platform — reducing energy use, enhancing comfort, and improving facility control.

Fire Suppression Systems

Expert design, installation & maintenance of advanced fire suppression systems (gas, foam, water mist, sprinklers) tailored to your facility.

Advanced Fire & Smoke Detection

We design, install, and maintain cutting-edge detection systems (addressable, conventional, aspirating) for rapid, reliable early warning.
